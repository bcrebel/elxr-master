    VISIT SITE

    PrettyTendr's founders were looking for a digital agency to create their vision for an innovative beauty platform. In a short turn around, elxr built a unique, custom-designed ecommerce and content application for the beauty startup. The application was web-based, eliminating the need for users to download a separate app, and responsive (or mobile-friendly) to delight visitors whether they were browsing the website on a smartphone, tablet or desktop. Lastly, the website was built on the Wordpress content management system, making it really easy for the PrettyTendr staff to frequently update the rich content.
